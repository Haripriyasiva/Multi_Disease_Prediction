A kidney-friendly diet limits sodium, potassium, and phosphorus.

- Control your protein intake.
- Choose fruits and vegetables that are low in potassium.
- Avoid processed foods high in sodium.
- Limit dairy products, which are high in phosphorus.
- Your doctor or a dietitian can help you create a personalized meal plan.
