A heart-healthy diet is low in sodium, saturated fats, and added sugars. It is rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

- Eat more fruits and vegetables.
- Choose whole grains like oats, brown rice, and quinoa.
- Eat fish high in omega-3 fatty acids, like salmon and mackerel.
- Limit red meat and processed meats.
- Avoid sugary drinks and snacks.
- Reduce your sodium intake by cooking at home and avoiding processed foods.
